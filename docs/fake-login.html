Training demo: Fake login (phishing). Back to list | Español
In this scenario (phishing): Check the real web address in the browser bar. Is it really your bank's domain? Did you type it or click a link from email/text? Real banks don't ask you to "verify" by logging in from a link. When in doubt, go to the bank's site by typing the URL yourself or using a saved bookmark. For emails that lead to fake logins, see Phishing emails. For how to read URLs and spot fake domains, see Typosquatting & reading URLs.
